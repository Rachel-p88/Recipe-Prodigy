# Recipe-Prodigy
Recipe Prodigy is a beginner friendly guide designed especially for university students and people who love to cook. It breaks food preparation into easy steps anyone can follow. It can recommend various recipes based on whatever ingredients you have. The user can give all the ingredients they have as an input. Recipe Prodigy suggests all the relevant recipes. The user can also rate and comment on the recipes. It’s useful to help other users decide which recipe works the best. There is also an option to sort the results based on the preferences of the user. Users can like the recipes to refer them later. It explains every step in plain words in the preferred language of the user. Recipe Prodigy promotes health by giving healthier recipes from the ingredients provided. It also suggests what else can be added to the recipe to make it appetizing.
